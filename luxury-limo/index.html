Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for standard services. For special events and weddings, we suggest booking 2-4 weeks ahead to ensure availability of your preferred vehicle.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

+

We monitor all flights in real-time. If your flight is delayed, we automatically adjust your pickup time at no additional charge. Your chauffeur will be waiting when you arrive.

Are your chauffeurs professionally trained?

+

All our chauffeurs undergo rigorous background checks, professional driving training, and customer service certification. They are licensed, insured, and committed to providing exceptional service.

Can I request specific amenities?

+

Absolutely. We offer customizable amenities including beverages, newspapers, child seats, and more. Simply let us know your preferences when booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Cancellations made more than 24 hours before scheduled service receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee depending on the service type.
